# Content Guide

## Tone

Write with architectural calm. Prefer clear explanation, public evidence and
bounded claims over persuasive language.

## Terminology

Use Enterprise Reality terminology consistently with approved publications.
Describe Enterprise Reality as the Constitutional Decision Platform for
regulated enterprises.

Do not position it primarily as a chatbot, CRM, ERP, workflow engine, data
platform or autonomous enterprise system.

## Capability Status

Use one of the approved capability statuses where maturity matters:

- Released
- In Development
- Planned
- Research
- Concept

Status must be shown as text, not colour alone.

## Claims Policy

Claims must be specific, supportable, current and proportionate. Do not invent
customers, adoption, partnerships, certification, commercial availability,
performance results or production outcomes.

## Headings

Use headings to expose structure. Keep one primary heading per page and preserve
a logical hierarchy.

## Diagrams

Diagrams must support understanding, remain readable without colour alone and
include accessible alternatives where published.

## Attribution

Approved publications should preserve publication ID, title, version, status,
author, date when known and canonical path. External sources require accurate
attribution and licensing review.

## Draft Handling

Draft collection entries may exist for schema validation or editorial work, but
they must not be routed into production output until approved.

## Prohibited Content

Do not include private source code, unpublished review discussion, internal
prompts, confidential correspondence, employer/client material, personal data,
credentials, secrets or unapproved publication drafts intended for release.
