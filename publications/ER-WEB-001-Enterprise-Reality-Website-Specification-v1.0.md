# Executive Position

The Enterprise Reality Website is not a marketing website.

It is the constitutional public presence of the Enterprise Reality
Programme.

Its purpose is to communicate the philosophy, architecture, research,
products and evolution of Enterprise Reality through truthful, governed
and evidence-based publications.

The website shall itself be developed using Constitution-Driven
Engineering (CDE) and shall reflect the same architectural discipline
that governs the Enterprise Reality Platform.

The website exists to help visitors understand Enterprise Reality before
asking them to adopt it.

---

# 1. Vision

To establish Enterprise Reality as the trusted public reference for
constitutional enterprise architecture, governed decision-making and
explainable enterprise automation.

The website shall provide a permanent, authoritative and publicly
accessible home for:

- Enterprise Reality architecture
- Research publications
- Product strategy
- Engineering methodology
- Release history
- Programme evolution

---

# 2. Objectives

Website v1.0 has six strategic objectives.

## 2.1 Explain Enterprise Reality

Provide a clear explanation of:

- why Enterprise Reality exists;
- the organisational problems it addresses;
- the constitutional philosophy that underpins it.

## 2.2 Publish Research

Provide a structured publication platform for:

- Enterprise Reality Research Series (ERRS)
- Architecture publications
- Strategy publications
- Executive briefings
- Release notes

## 2.3 Demonstrate Engineering Discipline

The website shall itself become a practical demonstration of
Constitution-Driven Engineering.

## 2.4 Support Commercial Engagement

Provide accurate information suitable for architects, technology
leaders, researchers, regulated organisations and prospective design
partners without exaggerating capability.

## 2.5 Preserve Programme History

Provide the permanent public record of publications, releases,
architectural evolution and programme milestones.

## 2.6 Enable Future Growth

Provide a foundation for future Decision Services, Explorer, Assistant,
Studio, demonstrations, documentation and learning resources while
clearly distinguishing planned capabilities from released capabilities.

---

# 3. Scope

Website v1.0 includes:

- Public website
- Publication platform
- Research library
- Architecture library
- Roadmap
- Release catalogue
- Industry scenarios

Website v1.0 excludes:

- Authentication
- SaaS capability
- Customer portals
- Live Runtime APIs
- Production Assistant
- Studio implementation
- Operational dashboards

---

# 4. Relationship to the Enterprise Reality Platform

The website is governed by Enterprise Reality.

It is not part of the Enterprise Reality Runtime.

The website publishes approved information, explains architecture,
communicates programme evolution and provides public access to approved
publications.

It does not perform reasoning, automation, evidence resolution or
execution.

---

# 5. Relationship to ER-CON-002

ER-CON-002 defines the constitutional principles governing the Website
Programme.

ER-WEB-001 defines the scope, objectives and deliverables of the Website
Programme.

Implementation guidance is provided through the Website Architecture
Library.

# 7. Success Criteria

Website v1.0 succeeds when it:

- accurately represents Enterprise Reality;
- preserves constitutional boundaries;
- distinguishes released, planned and research capabilities;
- supports public understanding;
- publishes approved research;
- demonstrates engineering discipline;
- provides a trustworthy foundation for future commercial engagement.

---

- Official public website
