# Chapter 1 - Why This Handbook Exists

## 1. Purpose

### Chapter Purpose

Explain why engineering knowledge should be preserved as a governed publication.

### Intended Learning Outcomes

- Identify the role of ER-WEB-005 within the Enterprise Reality Website publication set.
- Explain why architecture must survive its original architects.
- Explain why conversations are not a durable system of record.
- Recognise documentation as part of engineering practice.
- Understand how to use this handbook during authoring, review and maintenance.

### Chapter Boundary

This chapter defines the authoring frame for the handbook. It does not make new architectural arguments, introduce new engineering conclusions or replace the approved governing publications.
