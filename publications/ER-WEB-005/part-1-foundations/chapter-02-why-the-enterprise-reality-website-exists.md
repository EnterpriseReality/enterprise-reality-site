# Chapter 2 - Why the Enterprise Reality Website Exists

## 1. Purpose

### Chapter Purpose

Explain the constitutional role of the public website.

### Intended Learning Outcomes

- Identify the website's role as the public programme presence.
- Describe the website's publication and research responsibilities.
- Recognise the website's role in architecture communication.
- Explain the importance of trust and truthful representation.
- Distinguish the website repository from the private platform repository.
- Explain why the website is not the Enterprise Reality Runtime.

### Chapter Boundary

This chapter frames the website's public role. It does not redefine the Enterprise Reality Runtime, alter public/private repository boundaries or author new website architecture.
