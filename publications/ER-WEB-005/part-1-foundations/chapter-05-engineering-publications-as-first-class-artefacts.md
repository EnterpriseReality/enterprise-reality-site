# Chapter 5 - Engineering Publications as First-Class Artefacts

## 1. Purpose

### Chapter Purpose

Explain why publications are governed engineering assets.

### Intended Learning Outcomes

- Recognise publication lifecycle as a handbook topic.
- Recognise versioning as a handbook topic.
- Recognise traceability as a handbook topic.
- Recognise review and approval as handbook topics.
- Recognise publication metadata as a handbook topic.
- Recognise knowledge preservation as a handbook topic.
- Identify the future Enterprise Reality Engineering Library as a possible direction where supported by approved governance.
- Understand the relationship between ER-WEB-001 through ER-WEB-005.

### Chapter Boundary

This chapter frames publications as governed engineering assets. It does not create or approve ER-PUB-001, introduce a new publication family or author final conclusions about future library structure.
