# Publication Policy

This operational policy is derived from
[ER-WEB-004](publications/ER-WEB-004-Enterprise-Reality-Website-Publication-Guide-v1.0.md).
ER-WEB-004 remains the canonical publication guide.

## Repository Rules

- Public pages and content collections must use validated metadata.
- Draft content must not appear in production routes or generated output.
- Claims about capability, maturity, adoption, safety, performance or commercial
  availability must be traceable to approved public evidence.
- Material promoted from private programme work requires editorial review,
  architectural review, public classification and approval before publication.
- Public content must not expose private paths, unpublished review material,
  secrets, credentials or employer/client-specific information.
- Corrections that materially change meaning require review and a versioned
  record.

## Pre-Publication Checklist

- Editorial review completed.
- Architectural and constitutional review completed.
- Capability status is explicit where relevant.
- Metadata is complete and valid.
- Internal links resolve.
- Accessibility checks pass for affected routes.
- Claims are proportionate and evidence-based.
- Public/private boundary check has been run.
- Production build succeeds.
- Publication approval is recorded before deployment.

Deployment success does not constitute publication approval.
